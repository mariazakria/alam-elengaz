Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our building maintenance and natural stone services. We’re here to help you make informed decisions and ensure a smooth, hassle-free experience.

1. What types of building maintenance services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of maintenance services including waterproofing, crack repairs, façade cleaning, painting, and restoration of natural stone finishes.

2. Do you work with natural stone cladding and installation?

Yes, we specialize in installing and maintaining various types of natural stone, including marble, granite, travertine, and limestone for both indoor and outdoor use.

3. How long does a typical maintenance project take?

The duration depends on the scope of the project. Small repairs may take 1-3 days, while larger renovations could take 1-2 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline before starting.

4. Do you provide a warranty on your work?

Yes, we offer a warranty on all our services. The warranty period depends on the type of service provided, but we guarantee quality and long-lasting results.

5. Which areas do you cover?

We serve clients all across Cairo and surrounding governorates. For large-scale projects, we may also operate in other regions upon request.
